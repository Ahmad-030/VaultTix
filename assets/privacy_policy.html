Privacy Policy

How VaultTix handles your data

Last updated: January 2024
🌐 100% Offline
🚫 No Analytics
🔒 AES-256
📵 Zero Permissions
🗄️

Data Storage

All your data is stored exclusively on your device. Nothing is transmitted to external servers, cloud services, or third parties. Your notes, PIN, and encryption keys never leave your device.

🔒

Encryption

Every note is encrypted with AES-256-CBC before being written to storage. Your PIN is hashed using SHA-256 + unique salt. Even with direct storage access, your data remains fully protected.

🛡️

Zero Permissions

VaultTix requests no risky permissions — no camera, no contacts, no location, no internet. Biometric authentication (optional) is processed entirely by your device's secure enclave. No biometric data is ever stored by the app.

📊

No Analytics or Tracking

We collect zero analytics, usage data, crash reports, or telemetry of any kind. No third-party SDKs. No ad networks. What you do inside VaultTix stays completely private — always.

🎭

Dual PIN System

The Fake PIN opens a decoy vault to protect you under duress. Your real vault is only accessible via your real PIN or biometrics. Neither PIN is stored in plain text — both are hashed and salted.

📷

Screenshot Protection

VaultTix uses Android's FLAG_SECURE to prevent screenshots, screen recordings, and app previews in the recent apps switcher. Your content is never visually exposed.

🧒

Children's Privacy

VaultTix does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app has no account system, no data collection, and no network connectivity, making it inherently safe for all age groups.

📝

Policy Changes

Any updates to this Privacy Policy will be reflected in the app update notes. Since no data is collected, policy changes cannot affect stored user data.
